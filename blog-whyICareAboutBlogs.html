Why I care about blogs

June 19, 2024

It is 2024 and I am coming out as pro-blogging. That’s right, with countless social media platforms to choose from, I have chosen to put my thoughts on a webpage. Because I think blogs are awesome.

Every once in a while, I get an idea. And when I get ideas, I research. And if the idea is specific enough, then in the course of my research, I probably end up on some random person’s personal site where they talked about something related to this idea like one time. I absolutely love when this happens.

So this is the beginning of my blog. This specific post is for those people who, like me, have stumbled their way here on a whim. Hello! I’m glad you’re here. In this space, I will, on occasion, write about the things that I do, or that I care about enough that they deserve my attention for long enough to write one of these. If that’s the kind of thing you enjoy, take a scroll through. No pressure! Hope you enjoy.
